# 🌙 DreamSync

**Breathe with the Light. Walk the Dreamscape.**

DreamSync is an immersive WebXR-based VR wellness prototype designed to help users reduce stress and improve sleep quality through a calming virtual environment. By combining guided breathing, ambient sounds, and peaceful visuals, DreamSync creates a relaxing pre-sleep experience that promotes mindfulness and mental well-being.

🔗 **Live Demo:** https://rinkuham.github.io/dreamSync/

---

## 📌 Problem Statement

Sleep deprivation and stress have become increasingly common among students and professionals, affecting both physical and mental health. Many existing solutions lack immersion and fail to provide an engaging relaxation experience.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

DreamSync transforms relaxation into an immersive VR journey. Users enter a peaceful dreamscape where they can slow their breathing, disconnect from daily stress, and prepare for a restful sleep experience.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🌌 Immersive 3D dreamscape
- 🫁 Guided breathing prompts
- 🎵 Relaxing ambient soundscape
- 🥽 WebXR/VR headset support
- 🖥️ Desktop accessibility
- 🎮 Keyboard and mouse navigation
- 🔊 Sound toggle
- 👁️ Minimal, distraction-free interface
